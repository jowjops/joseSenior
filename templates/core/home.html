About College Saint Jean

For over three decades, College Saint Jean has been a beacon of educational excellence in Kherbet Qanafar, nurturing young minds and shaping future leaders through a holistic approach to education.

Our Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience that empowers students with knowledge, character, and skills to succeed in an ever-changing world.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in the region, recognized for academic excellence and the development of well-rounded, socially responsible individuals.

Our Values

Integrity, excellence, respect, and community engagement form the foundation of everything we do.
